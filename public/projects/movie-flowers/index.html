Cineflora: Visualizing Film Moods

Welcome to Cineflora, a creative coding project that visualizes movie genres as flowers!
Each flower represents a group of movie genres, with petal shapes and colors reflecting the mood.

Project inspired by Shirley Wu's Film Flowers project: https://sxywu.github.io/filmflowers/
